# Privacy Policy

## Data Collection

ClayModeller does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## Local Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:
- 3D models (.clay files) in app private storage
- Exported STL files in Downloads folder
- Auto-save files in app private storage
- App preferences

## Permissions

ClayModeller requests the following permissions:

### Storage (Android 9 and below)
- **Purpose**: Save and load 3D models, export STL files
- **Usage**: Only when you explicitly save/load/export
- **Data**: Model files only, no personal information

### No Internet Permission
ClayModeller does not have internet permission and cannot send data anywhere.

## Crash Reports

When the app crashes, you may be prompted to share a crash report. This is:
- **Optional**: You choose whether to share
- **Local**: Stored on your device until you share
- **Manual**: Shared via your email app
- **Content**: Technical crash information only (no personal data)

Crash reports are handled by ACRA (Application Crash Reports for Android) and stored locally.

## Third-Party Services

ClayModeller does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## GDPR Compliance

As ClayModeller does not collect any personal data, GDPR does not apply. All data remains on your device under your control.

## Children's Privacy

ClayModeler is safe for all ages as it does not collect any data.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be reflected in app updates.

## Contact

For privacy concerns, please open an issue on GitHub.
